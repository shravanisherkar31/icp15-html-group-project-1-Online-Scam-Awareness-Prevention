What is a Job Scam?

Job and internship scams are fraudulent job offers created to cheat job seekers by promising high salaries, guaranteed placement, or easy work-from- home opportunities. These scams mainly target students, fresh graduates, and unemployed individuals who are actively searching for jobs.

Scammers often pretend to be HR managers or recruiters from well-known companies and contact victims through emails, phone calls, WhatsApp messages, or social media platforms.
